## 📊 Case Study: Sales Data Analytics & Revenue Diagnostic Pipelines
* **Origin Framework:** Structured Practice via DataCamp Real-World Projects
* **Core Technology:** Alteryx Designer (Advanced ETL & Analytical Logic)

---

### 🔍 1. The Business Problem (The Context)
> "Manual substantive testing of high-volume revenue ledger entries introduces significant sampling risk and limits a corporate advisory team's ability to uncover hidden data operational patterns. For a diverse retail sales dataset containing multi-regional records, reliance on traditional basic spreadsheets makes it highly labor-intensive to isolate pricing anomalies, segment sales tiers, or identify transaction risks under tight close timelines."

### 🛠️ 2. Analytical Approach & Methodology
Using Alteryx Designer, I constructed an automated data pipeline to parse, join, and structure raw transactional details systematically:
1. **Data Integration & Cleaning:** Cleaned and parsed unstructured multi-source spreadsheets, managing data types and removing empty rows or invalid data points.
2. **Data Manipulation & Transformation:** Utilized **Join Tools** to combine regional datasets with lookup references and leveraged **Formula Tools** to calculate customized key performance financial metrics across transactional fields.
3. **Advanced Data Analysis:** Deployed **Filter Tools** to isolate high-value revenue outliers and **Summarize Tools** to calculate total units sold, regional trends, and category performance.
4. **Automated Reporting:** Streamlined the entire transactional analytics process into a single-click repeatable canvas template.
